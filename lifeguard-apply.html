Why Work at Hideaway Pool?

We're looking for responsible, motivated students and young adults to join our lifeguard team at Hideaway Pool in Hideaway Lakes, Texas.

It's more than a summer job. You'll learn skills that last a lifetime, work with a great team, and spend your summer outdoors doing meaningful work.

👋

Questions? Talk to Jennifer Borden (Head Manager) or Alex Brooks (Assistant Manager) at the pool, or send a message via the contact form.
