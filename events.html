🏊 Swim Lessons

4 one-hour classes - $75 total per child

Swim lessons are available for beginners and younger swimmers. Each class is one hour long and focuses on water safety and basic swimming skills in a fun, encouraging environment.

  • 🏅 Beginner & younger swimmers welcome
  • 🦺 Water safety focus
  • 🌊 Basic swimming skills
  • 👨‍🏫 Taught by certified lifeguards
  • ☀️ Small group sessions
  • ⌚ Tuesday-Friday 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
